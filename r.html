What is R?

R is a measure of the strength of a linear relationship that results in a value between -1 and 1. A value of -1 or 1 is a perfect linear relationship. A value of 0 indicates no linear relationship at all.

R is negative when the linear relationship is an inverse relationship (as one variable increases, the other decreases).
